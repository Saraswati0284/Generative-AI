# 🤖 Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence (AI)

## 📌 Overview
This repository contains my learning notes on the fundamental concepts of Artificial Intelligence.

---

## 🔹 Key Concepts

### 1. Artificial Intelligence (AI)
AI enables machines to perform tasks that require human intelligence such as decision-making and problem-solving.

### 2. Machine Learning (ML)
A subset of AI where systems learn from data and improve over time without being explicitly programmed.

### 3. Deep Learning
Uses neural networks with multiple layers to analyze complex patterns in data.

### 4. Natural Language Processing (NLP)
Allows machines to understand, interpret, and respond to human language.

### 5. Computer Vision
Enables machines to interpret and analyze visual data like images and videos.

### 6. Supervised Learning
Learning from labeled data (input with correct output).

### 7. Unsupervised Learning
Finding hidden patterns in data without labeled outputs.

### 8. Reinforcement Learning
Learning through rewards and penalties.

Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries and shaping the future. Understanding these fundamentals is the first step toward building a career in AI.
