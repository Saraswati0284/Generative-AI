# 🤖 Types of Artificial Intelligence

## 🧠 Based on Capability

### 🔹 Narrow AI (Weak AI)
- Performs specific tasks
- Examples: Siri, Google Assistant, Netflix recommendations

### 🔹 General AI (Strong AI)
- Human-level intelligence
- Can perform any intellectual task
- Not developed yet

### 🔹 Super AI
- Beyond human intelligence
- Theoretical concept

---

## ⚙️ Based on Functionality

### 🔹 Reactive Machines
- No memory
- Example: IBM Deep Blue

### 🔹 Limited Memory
- Learns from past data
- Used in self-driving cars

### 🔹 Theory of Mind
- Understands human emotions (future)

### 🔹 Self-aware AI
- Conscious AI (not real yet)

---

## 🧩 Based on Technology

### 🔹 Machine Learning
- Learns from data

### 🔹 Deep Learning
- Neural networks

### 🔹 NLP (Natural Language Processing)
- Understands human language

### 🔹 Computer Vision
- Works with images/videos

### 🔹 Generative AI
- Creates content (ChatGPT, DALL·E)

---

## 🎯 Summary
AI is categorized into:
- Capability
- Functionality
- Technology
